A highly motivated and ambitious Part 2 Architectural Graduate with a strong academic foundation and relevant professional experience. I am passionate about the complete architectural process, from concept and narrative development through to detailed resolution, with a particular interest in technically driven projects that require creative and elegant design solutions. I am proficient in both hand drawing and digital visualisation, with strong skills in physical model making, rendering, and architectural illustration. Known for a strong work ethic, a proactive mindset, and a commitment to continuous learning and collaboration.


During my professional career, I have contributed to the design development and preparation of planning documents, demonstrating attention to detail and a sound understanding of planning processes and UK building regulations.


I am especially drawn to narrative driven and speculative projects, approaching architecture as a physical manifestation of its surroundings, shaped by emotional, cultural, environmental, and human stories. My design ethos is rooted in the belief that architecture should not only respond to context but also provoke reflection, imagination, and meaningful connection.


I am currently seeking a full time Part 2 Architectural Assistant role where I can continue to grow as a designer and contribute to innovative, sustainable, and contextually grounded projects that positively shape the built environment.
